    Youth strides

    07 Jun 2012 » others

    From a very young age, I discovered my interest for sports. It all began in 1998 during a school cross-country competition where I placed 2nd. The first-place winner extended an invitation for me to join a club, which encouraged me to explore my athletic abilities for the next decade. This pursuit of sports went hand in hand with my academic pursuits until the end of university.

    This path provided me with valuable lessons in sportsmanship. I dedicated countless hours to practice and traveling across Portugal every week. I had a lot of fun during training camps and stays in various accommodations.

    As time went on, I started discovering new hobbies, and these memories began to fade.
