    Invitational system preview

    05 Jun 2023 » project

    Born out of the idea to create a quick demo of a versatile program for sharing information.

    When someone wants to share public information without it being permanently on the internet, like a business card, forming a “pizza team”, organizing a football game, or playing Dungeons and Dragons with friends, they can create a QR code to encapsulate that information. The system sets the information to expire after an hour.

    By scanning the QR code using a compatible reader, people can instantly access the associated content, eliminating the need for manual URL input or traditional sharing methods. Note that while there are easier ways to share information, the Invitational System focuses on demonstrating the convenience of QR codes. Although I am perfectly aware that there are easier ways to share information.

    It is not intended to be a full-fledged system for managing invitations or complex event registrations. Instead, it aims to serve as a proof-of-concept, demonstrating how QR codes can enhance the sharing experience.
